MyTMS — User Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: November 17, 2025

MyTMS respects your privacy. If you wish to have your data removed from MyTMS that was collected via Facebook Login, please follow the instructions below.

How to request deletion

To request deletion of all personal data associated with your Facebook login, email us at: ethiomisgie@gmail.com. Please include the Facebook account name or the email address connected to your Facebook account so we can identify your records.

What will be deleted

Upon confirmation, we will permanently delete:

  • Your MyTMS account (created via Facebook Login)
  • Stored profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Any session or authentication tokens associated with your account

Timing

We will acknowledge your request within 24 hours and complete deletion of your data within 48 hours after verification. If we cannot locate your account from the details provided, we will contact you to request additional verification information.

Automatic deletion on app removal

If you remove or uninstall the MyTMS app from your Facebook account, Facebook will notify us and we will automatically remove any data associated with your Facebook login within the same 48-hour timeframe.

Contact for deletion requests or questions:
Email: ethiomisgie@gmail.com
Note: This page is provided to satisfy Facebook Developer requirements for a User Data Deletion callback/information page. If you intend to implement a machine-readable deletion callback endpoint (for automated deletion), include that endpoint URL in your app settings.
