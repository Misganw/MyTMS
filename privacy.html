Privacy Policy for MyTMS

Last updated: November 17, 2025

Thank you for using MyTMS. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our application and Facebook Login.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information from Facebook Login

When you sign in with Facebook, and only with the permissions you consent to, we may collect:

  • Your full name
  • Your Facebook email address
  • Your Facebook profile picture
  • Your Facebook ID (used for authentication)

We do not collect or store your Facebook password.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect technical data such as browser type, device information, IP address and log data for security and performance monitoring.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for these purposes only:

  • User authentication and secure login
  • Create and manage your MyTMS account
  • Provide and improve application features
  • Security, fraud prevention, and analytics
  • Communicating with you about account issues when necessary

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes.

We may disclose information only if required by law, to comply with legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of MyTMS and our users.

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures intended to protect your data. While we strive to protect your information, no security system is impenetrable.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide services and for legitimate business purposes. If you delete your account or request deletion, we will remove associated data as described on our Data Deletion page.
